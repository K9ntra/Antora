# Funcs & Stuff

Welcome to the official **Funcs & Stuff** repository!

## What is Funcs & Stuff?

Funcs & Stuff is a script hub designed for games on the Roblox platform. It allows players to discover and enjoy new ways to experience games beyond standard gameplay.

## Our Goal

Our goal is to democratize game customization on the platform by enabling all players to access tools that enhance and expand their gaming experience.
